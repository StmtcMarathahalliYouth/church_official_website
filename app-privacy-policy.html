Privacy Policy  STMTC App

Last updated: 4th September 2025

St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church Marathahalli ("we," "our," or "us") built the STMTC App to help members of our congregation stay connected with church activities and each other. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

The STMTC App contains a church directory with basic member information, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Birthday and anniversary dates

This information is provided by church members and maintained by the church administration. The app itself does not collect any additional data such as location, contacts, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

The information in the directory is used for:

  • Helping parishioners connect with each other
  • Sharing church announcements, schedules, and events
  • Providing contact details for prayer groups and leadership

We do not and will not sell, trade, or rent this information to third parties.

3. Access to Information

  • The directory is accessible only to registered members of the church.
  • OTP (On registered email id) based login is required to access member information.
  • The app does not allow in-app messaging, but users may contact each other outside the app using the shared details.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate steps to protect personal information, including:

  • Restricted access through member login
  • Secure hosting of data
  • Limited access for administrators

However, please note that no system can be 100% secure.

5. User Rights

Church members can:

  • Request updates or corrections to their personal details
  • Request removal of their information from the directory by contacting the church office

6. Children's Privacy

The STMTC App is intended for use by all church members. Content is only uploaded and moderated by the Church Secretary and Vicar.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app.
