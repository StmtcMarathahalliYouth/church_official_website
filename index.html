Vicar's Message

Shaiju P John

Vicar

Praise to His love that carried us through the last year! And, great is the Grace that has led us to this New Year!

Though we may not be certain what this year has in store for us, we need not be anxious as our trust is in the Lord who is certain of all things

Let his Grace and guidance carry us through this year

Let us live this little life that God has gifted us with joy, love, care and respect towards one another, that His name be glorified through us. Then, will the Lord God Almighty delight in us.

What great desire should a Man have, other than to please God?!

With prayers,

Rev. Shaiju P John
